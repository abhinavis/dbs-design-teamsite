AVP, Design Producer — Institutional Banking Group

Design producers guide design teams to better ways of working through the right tools and processes. They know what the team needs before they themselves do. And they advocate in the organisation to make it happen.

What you'll be doing

Your goal will be to enable the design team to do better work. You will dig deep to find out why things don't work, fight hard to get rid of outdated processes, find ways to make our money go further and bring harmony to the team. Because of you, our designers will have the space to think, breathe and create.

Here's what your week might look like

  • Your morning coffee will be accompanied by catching up on emails and Slack messages. Collaborating with many different teams and departments across the organisations means you will have a lot of correspondence to keep up with.

  • Budgeting is never going to be far from your mind. You will constantly assess the team's expenses and find ways to make our money go further, so that we can get the things we need.

  • At a company as large as DBS, there always is a process that can be more efficient. We will look to you for ways to work more effectively and happily. This role is very much about strategy. Constantly thinking of new ways to work and collaborate.

  • People are happy when they are focused and know that their day-to-day work is directly contributing to the team's goals. So we would love you to help us measure our performance and track our tasks better.

  • Morning, day or night, you will be the go-to person when someone doesn't know how something works. You will be our very own design operations oracle!

Here are the experience and skills we'd love you to have

  • You have 5-8 years of project management experience and a passion for design.

  • You are good with numbers and can find your way around Excel.

  • You are an expert communicator with a good deal of diplomacy skills and the ability to communicate complex ideas.

  • Commercial awareness with an eye for seeing business improvement opportunities.

Bonus

  • You have experience in the fintech industry.

  • You enjoy planning family events and trips.

  • De-cluttering gives you joy!

Your team and career

This role sits within Design Operations, one of the units in the Center of Excellence team, and is based in Singapore. This is a regional group-level role, which means you will work with Consumer and Corporate banking (Institutional Banking Group), and Enterprise (internal tools).

You will focus largely on the Corporate banking design team, where they support business banking platforms on a global scale. The team will drive projects from start to finish and you will support them in tooling and defining processes. You will report to the Head of Design Operations with the potential to grow a team.

About DBS

DBS is a leading financial services group in Asia. We are headquartered in Singapore, with a growing presence in Greater China, Southeast Asia and South Asia.

We were named the Best Bank in the World by Global Finance in August 2018—the first Singapore and Asian bank to receive this honour.

We want our customers to Live more, Bank less and we want to make banking invisible. Every day, there’s a lot more to explore, experience and enjoy—and a lot less time to do it in. That’s why we’ve created banking that’s fast, natural, effortless. Banking that’s with you wherever life takes you, instead of taking you away from life. So you can enjoy a lot more life, with a lot less bank.
