Get to know us

a little better

From 2 to 60
in five years

In 2014 we had two designers. Fast-forward to 2020 and there are more than 60 of us. We’ve gone from wearing banker-like suits (and feeling very uncomfortable) to jeans and t-shirts, winning the ‘war’ in using Macs and having a design studio in our office. We've truly infiltrated the world of banking.

 

Our strategy has always been simple — deliver high quality work to help other teams understand the value of design.

 

We are not brilliant jerks and moody superstars. We are get s*** done people. We are a team that perseveres, commits to long-term goals and delivers real business value.

01

Curiosity over certainity

We strive to keep an open mind, and to seek diverse viewpoints. This means that we share our knowledge, time and attention with each other generously.

We also know we can be wrong, and aren't afraid to admit it.

Our values

These are the words we live by on an everyday basis so we know where at boy

02

Step up & step in

We step up and step in to help others. We take initiative to see a project through (even if it's not exactly in our job description).

If something needs fixing, we do our best to fix it.

03

One band, one sound

We want our work to make an impact. We can do that when we work together in a deliberate, consistent way. The result is a coherent experience, both for our customers and internal stakeholders.

This doesn't mean we're all the same: the band needs different instruments to sound interesting.

04

Connect & Celebrate

We're all whole people and we all have lives outside of work. We make the effort to get to know each other, to celebrate one another, and to build real connections within our team.

If you're having a bad day, that's okay. Here, have some chocolate and a hug.

05

Grit

We're building something new, and that's never easy. We know that success takes hard work, and we have the patience to see things through.

It's also important to strike a balance. Sometimes we need to pick our battles, and that's okay, too.

All of us
